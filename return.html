Return Policy

At Grameen Cooling Solution, customer satisfaction is our priority. Our return policy is as follows:

  • Returns are accepted within 7 days of service if there is a valid issue.
  • All return requests must be accompanied by the original order ID.
  • Services performed cannot be returned but issues will be addressed promptly.
  • For product returns, items must be unused and in original packaging.
  • Please contact our support team before initiating a return.

For assistance, please contact us.
